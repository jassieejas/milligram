THE STORY
OF MILIGRAM

On a normal day, you'll find Kilo & Gram the 2 cats sleeping at the doorstep, proclaiming their bed under the ourdoor woodshed palette while you gracefully sip your cup of coffee in the cozy little cafe. This little resident cat has been here ever since the launch of this homely cafe.

Opening 7 days a week, serving an all day menu with a myriad of homemade cakes, sandwiches, serious food and of course, not to forget their signature Earl Grey Latte and T2 teas that is sure to find you dropping a sweet tooth or two.

Find your escape from this fussy hectic city with a warm cup in your hand and a cute little cat on your lap that makes you feel at H.O.M., else you can always find a baristas in duty for a good little chat while you are slouching away on a lazy afternoon.

Be sure to come visit us that also provide our space for events, catering and food delivery!.
